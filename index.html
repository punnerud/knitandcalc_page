About Knit&Calc

Knit&Calc is the ultimate app for everyone who loves to knit! Keep track of your yarn stash, organize projects, save patterns, and use smart calculators that make knitting easier.

üìã Projects

üìñ Patterns

Save patterns as PDF, images or website links. Organize in categories (sweaters, pants, hats, socks, mittens, or custom). Search and filter among your patterns and connect them directly to projects.
