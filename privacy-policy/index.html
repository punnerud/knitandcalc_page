Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Auto Mouse Jiggler Mover handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

App Permissions

Auto Mouse Jiggler Mover requires permission to control your mouse cursor to perform the jiggling function. All processing happens directly on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit any data to external servers.

Information Collection and Use

Our app collects anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app experience. This data cannot be used to identify individual users and includes basic information such as app launches and feature usage.

Data Storage

Any app preferences or settings are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any user accounts or store personal information on external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
